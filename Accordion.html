HTML Example:

        <div class="accordion-item">
            <button class="product__category--accordion">CONIFERS</button>
            <div class="product__category__content">
                <p>
                    Conifers are cone-bearing, mostly evergreen trees and shrubs
                    known for their needle-like or scale-like leaves. They
                    reproduce via cones, with male cones producing pollen and
                    female cones containing seeds. These trees play vital roles
                    in ecosystems, providing habitat, stabilizing soil, and
                    serving as a valuable source of wood. Common examples
                    include pines, spruces, and firs, prized for their economic
                    and ornamental value.
                </p>
            </div>
        </div>
        
        <div class="accordion-item">
            <button class="product__category--accordion l-mb-0_5">EVERGREEN</button>
            <div class="product__category__content">
                <p>
                    Evergreen refers to plants, mostly trees and shrubs, that
                    retain their foliage throughout the year, unlike deciduous
                    plants that shed their leaves seasonally. These plants often
                    have adaptations like needle-shaped leaves or thick waxy
                    coatings to conserve water. Evergreens provide year-round
                    color and texture to landscapes, offer shelter for wildlife,
                    and can serve as windbreaks and privacy screens. They are
                    popular in landscaping for their aesthetic appeal and low
                    maintenance requirements. Examples include pine, cedar, and
                    holly trees.
                </p>
            </div>
        </div>
